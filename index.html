Medics

Doctor Incision

Armed with expert precision, Dr. Incision throws razor-sharp scalpels with surgical accuracy, cutting down infections before they spread.

Doctor Molar

Once a passionate dentist, Dr. Molar discovered a secret technique to weaponize extracted teeth. Now, he launches reinforced molars at germs, causing devastating impact damage.

Doctor Sweet

Known for making kids smile, Dr. Sweet’s secret weapon is her arsenal of sugar-coated medicine. She fires candy-coated cures that damage germs while energizing allies.

Nurse Vital

A frontline warrior of the medical field, Nurse Vital doesn’t just heal—she fights back! Using specialized vaccines, she neutralizes germs while strengthening allies.

Doctor Rx

Dr. Rex once worked in a quiet pharmacy, but now, he's on the battlefield, deploying automated dispensers that distribute pills to attack germs.

Doctor Smooth

A skincare expert, Dr. Smooth realized that specially formulated medical lotions can slow and weaken germs. His secret formula leaves infections struggling to spread.

About Us

Welcome to Germageddon

Not all warriors carry swords — some carry stethoscopes

The name “Germageddon” Inspired by the term Armageddon, meaning the site of a final, decisive battle between good and evil, Germageddon envisions a world where the fight for survival takes place in the halls of a hospital under siege.

One of our core mission is to be able to blend education and entertainment through interactive and interesting gameplay that raises awareness about real-world health problems. Will you rise as the hero of the healthcare frontline? Join us at Germageddon, and help turn the tide in the battle for a healthier tomorrow!
